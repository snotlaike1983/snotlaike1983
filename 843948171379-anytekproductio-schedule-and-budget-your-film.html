About:

Please contact me first

Every project is different and I need to know more about it to give you a custom quote.

If an order is made without getting a quote, I will look through your script and give you a detailed quote (minus the $5 already paid) and send you the custom offer. If the offer is not accepted, to avoid a negative completion rate on my account, the $5 will not be refunded and will go towards the time spend drafting your quote.

  • Day out of Days (DOOD) reports for Cast, Extras, Vehicles & and more

  • Film Business Plans
  • Film Finance Plans
  • Sales Projections
